How to catch a unicorn

You have found yourself wondering: "What would it take to catch a unicorn?" Perhaps it would take the strength of Super Woman, x-ray vision or viking-like courage to brave treacherous oceans? Luckily, you don't need any of that. We have developed tools that make your quest possible even without leaving your computer.

How does it work?

We provide you with the following 3 tools:

  • ulisten: Search the world for a unicorn heartbeat. Each unicorn has a unique heartbeat signature.
  • ulocate: Get the GPS coordinates of your unicorn's location.
  • greetNmeet: Greet and meet your unicorn online, if you don't feel like traveling,

Before getting started

Make sure you have everything you need:

  • You will need a GitHub Account.
  • You will need a CI that runs sh scripts. Windows users click here.
  • Swivel chair (optional)

Quickstart guide

  1. Clone the unicorn repository on GitHub to your local computer.

    git clone https://github.com/evelachief/unicorn

  2. On your local computer switch to the unicorn directory.

    cd unicorn

  3. Start searching the world for a unicorn by running the script ulisten.

    sh ulisten

    If a unicorn is found, you are able to observe the unicorn's unique heartbeat.

    After 5 minutes the search runs into a timeout. That indicates all existing unicorns are sleeping. While sleeping, the unicorn's heartbeat signature is too soft for us to detect. Just try again in one hour!

  4. Locate the unicorn by running the script ulocate.

    sh ulocate

    You just located your first unicorn. Grab your coat and buy a train ticket to the printed location. Don't delay if possible. If you are short on time or don't feel like travelling, don't worry and continue with the next step.

  5. Start spinning in your swivel chair. Choose your pace wisely.

    You may feel a bit funny, but that's perfectly normal..

  6. While spinning run the script greetnmeet:

    sh greetnmeet

    Note that typing while spinning is way more fun than copying+pasting.

    Say hello. It's magic.

What is a unicorn?

A unicorn is a mythical creature. It looks like a horse with a spiralled horn (or "alicorn" according to medieval lore) on its forehead. You find unicorns often depicted in close proximity to star dust or rainbows. Legend has it that their heartbeat has a unique signature.
